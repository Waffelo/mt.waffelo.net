Rules üìú



The server rules are tailored to make the game experience more comfortable to others. These rules have few categories, and those are general, building, social, economy and PVP. There are no exceptions for these rules.

General:

  1. Use common sense.
  2. Keep your eyes on the rules, they may change.
  3. Do not intentionally ruin the fun for other players.
  4. Do not use hacked clients, hacks, bugs or glitches to gain an advantage over other players.

Building:

  1. Do not grief or alter other player builds.
  2. You are not allowed to build above or underneath other player builds.
  3. You area not allowed to build too close to other player's buildings/ Minimal distance is 50 blocks.
  4. Do not build one-use ugly structures that destroy the aesthetics of the world. After building a dirt hut to wait trough the night for example, you must destroy it.
  5. Do not build structures that lag the server.

Social:

  1. Harrassment is strictly prohibited.
  2. Do not create unnecessary drama.
  3. Do not swear excessively.
  4. Racist, Homophobic or any other slurs are strictly prohibited.
  5. Spamming or flooding the chat is not allowed.
  6. Do not reveal your or other player's personal information.

Economy:

  1. You are not allowed to scam other players while trading or selling items.
  2. Do not use alternate accounts or glitches/bugs to farm money.

PvP:

  1. Do not kill new players at spawn.
  2. If a player asks you to stop killing them, you must obey.
